Hey there!

I'm Nabiha — currently pursuing diploma in informatics library at UiTM Kedah. I'm a quiet thinker, emotional dreamer, and someone who finds beauty in the softest corners of the world. I’m all about tiny joys: vintage playlists, handwritten notes, and rainy days by the window.

Things I love doing ♡

Spending quiet mornings journaling. Reading my comfort books. Enjoy playing with my console game. I love slow living, playlists that speak to me, and walking aimlessly with my PSP.

The Middle Soul

I'm the second child — not the eldest leader, not the youngest baby, but somewhere in-between. I’m the quiet observer, the peacekeeper, the one who’s soft but steady. I feel everything, but I don’t always say it out loud.

Things I’m Hoping For

I want to travel alone and out from my comfort zone, even just once — maybe Kyoto, maybe Amsterdam. I want to start a personal zine and leave tiny love notes for strangers. I want my own little cafe, no matter how small. I want to live romantically even in the most mundane days.

My Fashion Journal

Soft knits, oversized sleeves, vibrant tones, and vintage finds — that's how I tell stories without speaking. My fashion journal is a collection of moods, feelings, and colours I wear to match the way I feel inside. Dressing up is my way of journaling.
